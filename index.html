About Me

I am a motivated and enthusiastic software engineer who is looking to automate workflow processes within a workplace. I graduated from UC San Diego and have a tax background from working in public accounting for the past 4 years. I found my passion for software engineering through the implementation of workflow automation within the tax department. As a consumer of tax/accounting softwares, there were always flaws within each software that I personally wanted to make better. I decided to leave public accounting to begin my journey to becoming a software engineer so that I can actually create software applications that automate workflow processes.

I am currently working as an Associate Instructor of Software Engineering at Hack Reactor @ Galvanize.
